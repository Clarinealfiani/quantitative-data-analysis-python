# Inferential Statistic

Inferential statistics is a method used for find out the population based on samples by analyzing and interpret data into a conclusion. Statistics inferential aims to draw conclusions. Before drawing conclusions, an assumption is made can be obtained from descriptive statistics. Inferential statistics were used to draw conclusions from several people, events, and times for the whole (generalization). The properties of inferential statistics, namely: (1) the data analyzed comes from random sampling (random); (2) generalize and predict well about the important characteristics of something variables and relationships between variables; (3) generalization and prediction made is applied to the entire population on the basis of results analysis of data from samples; and (4) generalizations and predictions are carried out by testing hypotheses or checking assumptions.
